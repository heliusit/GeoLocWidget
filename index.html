GeoLocWidget

Geolocation Widget uses autocomplete, reverse geocoding, and Google Maps.
GeoLocWidget was developed to resolve licensing issues with Google Maps (like show map when use it), and it helps to resolve request limit issues through the use of several geo providers like MapQuest, OpenStreetMap, and Google Maps.
